Historical Places

Lauterbrunnen

A unique village situated in the Swiss Alps that is full of sensational rocky cliffs and a bonus of the 300 meter-high Staubbach Falls, plus the 72 waterfalls in Lauterbrunnen Valley

Interlaken

An amzing resort town that is located between Lake Brienz and Lake Thun. It has plenty of acres of flower gargens, peaks of mountains and open landscapes.It comes with plenty of activities to do scuch as kayaking, hiking, abseling,skiing, paragliding and snowboarding.It also has 45 modes of different transport

Bern

A wonderful old town that has the tallest cathedral in Switzerland.It also consists of beautiful meseums and galleries. Its a must visit for people who love art.
